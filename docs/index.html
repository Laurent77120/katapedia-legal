Katapedia

Android app dedicated to karate practice.
Katapedia is an encyclopedia of katas and techniques used in Shotokan karate.
The full description and list of features are available on the Google Play page.

Business model

The app is free to download and provides access to two katas. A one-time purchase unlocks all content.

Developer

Developed by Laurent Gaillard, a karate practitioner.
